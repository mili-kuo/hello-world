🍉 Refreshing Watermelon Fruit Salad Recipe

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Chill Time: 30 minutes | Serves: 6-8 people

Description

This refreshing watermelon fruit salad is the perfect summer treat! Combining fresh, juicy watermelon with other complementary fruits and a light lime-mint dressing, it's healthy, delicious, and ideal for picnics, potlucks, or hot days when you need something cool and hydrating.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole watermelon (about 8-10 lbs), cubed or balled
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, halved
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey or agave nectar
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Optional: 1/4 cup shredded coconut or feta cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cut the watermelon in half and remove the seeds. Using a melon baller or knife, cut the watermelon into cubes or round balls. Place in a large serving bowl.
  2. Add blueberries, strawberries, pineapple chunks, and raspberries to the bowl with the watermelon.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, honey, chopped mint, and sea salt.
  4. Pour the lime-mint dressing over the fruit and gently toss to combine.
  5. If using, sprinkle coconut or crumbled feta cheese over the top.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld and chill.
  7. Stir gently before serving and add more fresh mint for garnish if desired.

Tips

  • Choose a watermelon that feels heavy for its size and has a creamy yellow spot where it sat on the ground—this indicates ripeness.
  • For a tropical twist, add fresh mango chunks or kiwi slices.
  • If you prefer it sweeter, add an extra tablespoon of honey or a splash of sweet dessert wine.
  • This salad keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 2 days in an airtight container.
  • For a fancy presentation, serve in a hollowed-out watermelon shell!
  • Make it a dessert by drizzling with a light balsamic reduction.

Variations

  • Spicy Watermelon: Add a pinch of chili powder or cayenne pepper for a sweet and spicy kick.
  • Protein Boost: Add hemp seeds, chia seeds, or Greek yogurt dollops for extra nutrition.
  • Cocktail Version: Mix in a splash of coconut rum or Aperol for an adult beverage.
  • Simple Version: Just watermelon, lime juice, and mint for the minimalist approach.
